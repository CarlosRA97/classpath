There is currently only one general discussion list for Classpath. To subscribe, send a message with a subject of "subscribe" to classpath-request@gnu.org. To unsubscribe, send a message to the same address with a subject of "unsubscribe" to classpath-request@gnu.org. To send a message to the list, mail it to classpath@gnu.org.

If you have any problems subscribing to the list, please send an email to rao@gnu.org.

The archive section is being revamped to hopefully segragate messages by month. Until then, you can read the archive here.
